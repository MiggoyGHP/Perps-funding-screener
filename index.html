Conventions & caveats

  • Annualization is simple, not compounded — APR = per-interval rate × intervals per year, the desk convention for quoting funding.
  • Intervals are per-symbol data. Binance's HYPEUSDT settles every 4h while BTCUSDT settles every 8h; both venues' majority interval is now 4h. This page reads the interval per market from the venue itself and shows it next to every rate.
  • Identical Hyperliquid rates are real. HL funding is premium + clamp(interest − premium, ±0.05%) with interest fixed at 0.01%/8h — when premium sits inside the band, the usual state for majors in a calm market, funding pins at exactly 0.00125%/1h. Rows showing it carry a floor chip; per-asset differentiation appears precisely when carry appears.
  • Predicted ≠ settled. Displayed rates are each venue's estimate for the upcoming settlement (consistent basis across venues); realized funding will differ.
  • OIL (xyz:CL) is a HIP-3 builder-deployed synthetic on Hyperliquid (trade.xyz): deployer-set oracle, 0.5 funding multiplier already inside the quoted rate, and no investable spot leg — so no carry verdict is offered.
  • Read-only demonstration. No execution, no accounts, no keys. Nothing here is investment advice.
